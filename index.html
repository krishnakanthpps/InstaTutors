    Who are we?

    InstaTutors is a platform created by students to help students who need tutoring connect with tutors, schedule tutoring sessions, and receive the help they need in all of their subjects.

    All InstaTutors tutoring sessions happen over video call, which gives the possibility of connecting students from all over the world.

    Our mission is to provide better tutoring solutions to students from any background, anywhere.

    More About Us

    How it Works

    1. Create an account, and verify your email.
    2. Log in, and make a tutoring request. Browse our tutors on our tutors page, or use our "find tutors" feature.
    3. You will be notified when a tutor accepts your request, and your tutor will email you shortly after.
    4. Your tutor will send you a link to a private video chat room on appear.in. Simply join when it comes time for your FREE tutoring session!
      5. Get Started

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the no-show policy?

    Your account will be terminated if you do not show up to 2 tutoring sessions.

    Can I request a certain tutor?

    Yes, and we can help you find a perfect tutor based on the subjects you need help in! (Go to "My Account")

    Can I sign up for 2 requests on the same day?

    No, and if you do, it will overwrite the first request.

    Do you have any more questions?

    Send us your question and we'll answer it ASAP.
